Our Story

Jericho Homestead is an independent, nongovernmental, nonprofit organization founded by five siblings who inherited farmland from their late father. The organization's mission is to provide a home for the homeless and to help those in need.

The inspiration for Jericho Homestead began with a powerful dream one of the siblings had at the age of 11. In the dream, he encountered an elderly man who resembled a beggar. The boy asked, "Where are you going?" The man responded, "I have nowhere to go." He then collapsed into the boy's arms and passed away.

Perplexed and moved, the boy pointed to his family's land and declared in his dream:
"When I am older, I will build a home for the homeless."

Life went on, but the dream stayed with him. Twenty-two years later, after becoming a spiritual leader, the same dream returned—exactly as he had experienced it as a child. This powerful message reaffirmed the call to build Jericho Homestead.
