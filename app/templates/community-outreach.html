Our Mission

A Guiding Light for the Less Fortunate

Jericho Homestead is here to empower the homeless population in India. With an average of 0.15% of the population experiencing homelessness, we recognize the urgency to address this issue. Our community outreach programs aim to provide humanitarian efforts for the elderly and young homeless individuals.

Our organization has been operating since 2018, and our staff is dedicated to helping students from low-income households who don't receive adequate resources. We provide education, clothing, and food to help move the less fortunate out of poverty and lead a more sustainable lifestyle.

At Jericho Homestead, we believe in being a guiding light for the less fortunate. Our available programs and tools are designed to teach them the skills they need to succeed.

Our Comprehensive Programs

Hunger Prevention Programs

Providing nutritious meals and food security to ensure no one goes hungry. We work to address immediate hunger needs while building long-term food security solutions.

Housing Programs

Building and staffing homes to offer the homeless a safe and secure environment. We provide shelter and create stable living conditions for those in need.

Stability Programs

Teaching essential life skills such as accessing health services and becoming self-sufficient in providing for their families. We focus on creating lasting stability.

Educational Programs

Providing education, clothing, and resources to help students from low-income households succeed academically and build a brighter future.

Breaking the Cycle of Poverty

Through our community outreach initiatives, we strive to break the cycle of poverty and create a brighter future for those in need. By building and staffing homes, we offer the homeless a safe and secure environment while teaching them the skills they need to succeed.
