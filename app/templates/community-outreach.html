Community Outreach

Jericho Homestead empowers India’s homeless—elders and youth alike—through targeted programs that break the cycle of poverty and build sustainable futures.

Our Comprehensive Programs

Hunger Prevention Programs

Daily meal distributions and nutrition education ensure no one goes to bed hungry.

Housing Programs

We build and staff safe, secure homes that provide dignity and stability.

Stability Programs

Counseling, healthcare access, and life-skills training help individuals regain self-sufficiency.

Educational Programs

Schooling support, tutoring, and vocational training open doors for children and adults alike.

A Guiding Light for the Less Fortunate

Since 2018 our dedicated staff has provided education, clothing, and food to students from low-income households. Our tools and programs equip them with the skills they need to thrive and build sustainable livelihoods.
